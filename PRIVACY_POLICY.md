# Privacy Policy - TalentSphere Speed Dial

**Last updated: November 10, 2025**

## Overview

TalentSphere Speed Dial is a Chrome extension designed for TalentSphere employees to access company resources and manage personal bookmarks. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.**

The extension does not:
- Track your browsing history
- Collect analytics or usage statistics
- Share data with third parties
- Use cookies or tracking technologies
- Transmit any personal information to external servers

## Data Storage

### Company Speed Dials
- Fetched from a secure GitHub repository managed by TalentSphere
- Cached locally in Chrome's storage
- Updated automatically every 7 days
- No personal data is included in company speed dials

### Personal Data
- Personal shortcuts and custom groups are stored locally using Chrome's sync storage
- Data remains on your device and syncs only through your Google Chrome account
- We have no access to your personal shortcuts
- You can export/delete your data at any time through the extension settings

## Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following permissions:

- **storage**: Save your preferences, personal shortcuts, and cached company speed dials
- **alarms**: Schedule automatic updates for company speed dials every 7 days
- **tabs**: Open bookmarked links and detect the current page when using "Add to Speed Dials"
- **contextMenus**: Add right-click menu options for quickly adding bookmarks

## Third-Party Services

The extension fetches company speed dial data from:
- GitHub Pages (https://phumphreyNEW-302.github.io/talentsphere-speed-dials/)
- This is a static JSON file hosted on GitHub's infrastructure
- No personal data is transmitted during this fetch

Favicon images are fetched from:
- Google's favicon service (https://www.google.com/s2/favicons)
- Only the website URL is sent to retrieve the icon
- No personal data is transmitted

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device
- Chrome's built-in sync uses encryption
- Company speed dial updates use HTTPS
- No passwords or sensitive information are stored

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Export your personal shortcuts at any time (Settings → Data Management → Export Data)
- Delete all personal data by removing the extension
- Opt out of using the extension at any time

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
